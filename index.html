ABOUT APECX 2025

The Annual Petroleum Exhibition and Competition (APECX), organized by the Society of Petroleum Engineers Universitas Gadjah Mada Student Chapter (SPE UGM-SC), is the largest annual event hosted by the organization. In 2024, APECX was successfully held with the theme "Enhancing The Contribution of Oil and Gas for Future Energy Resiliensy" The event featured a range of competitions and activities, including pre-events, main events, and post-events, which garnered significant enthusiasm from professionals and participants alike. Key highlights included the Geothermal Competition, Petrosmart Competition, Paper and Poster Competition, Mud Innovation, Mangrove Social Project, Charity Program, SC Gathering, and Awarding Night. The competitions aimed to provide a platform for participants to enhance their skills and knowledge in the petroleum sector. Additionally, the event series offered valuable opportunities for students with an interest in petroleum and energy issues to engage and learn. Through APECX, the organizers aspired to foster networking and collaboration among participants and professionals, facilitating the exchange of ideas and contributing to advancements in the petroleum industry.
