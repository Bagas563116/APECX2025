STUDENT CHAPTER GATHERING

The SPE Student Chapter Gathering is a dynamic event that brings together SPE Student Chapters from various universities. This gathering serves as a platform for students to connect, exchange ideas, and expand their knowledge in the energy and petroleum industry.

During the event, participants will have the opportunity to attend an insightful seminar delivered by a distinguished speaker, providing valuable perspectives on current trends and challenges in the field. Beyond the seminar, the gathering fosters meaningful discussions, allowing students to engage in knowledge-sharing, networking, and collaboration.

This event aims to strengthen the bonds between SPE Student Chapters, enhance professional development, and inspire the next generation of industry leaders.
