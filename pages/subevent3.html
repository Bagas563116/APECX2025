COMPANY VISIT

As part of the prestigious annual event APECX 2025, the Society of Petroleum Engineers Universitas Gadjah Mada Student Chapter (SPE UGM SC) proudly organizes the Company Visit program. This initiative serves as a valuable opportunity for students, particularly those pursuing studies in petroleum engineering, geosciences, and other energy-related fields, to gain firsthand exposure to the professional environment of the oil and gas industry.

The Company Visit aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry practices by allowing students to directly observe how leading companies operate, understand the latest technological advancements, and engage in meaningful discussions with professionals in the field.

Each year, the Company Visit is carefully designed to provide participants with an in-depth understanding of various aspects of the industry, including upstream, midstream, and downstream operations. Students will have the opportunity to explore cutting-edge technologies, safety protocols, and business strategies implemented by industry leaders.

By engaging in direct interactions with professionals, participants will gain valuable insights into career opportunities, industry challenges, and the skills required to thrive in this dynamic sector. This experience not only enhances their academic knowledge but also fosters critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a deeper appreciation of industry standards and best practices.

Furthermore, this initiative strongly aligns with the United Nations‚Äô Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 4: Quality Education, by promoting experiential learning and facilitating knowledge transfer between industry experts and future professionals. Through exposure to real-world industry practices, students are better equipped to apply theoretical concepts in practical settings, making them more prepared for their future careers. Additionally, the program encourages discussions on sustainability, energy transition, and the role of oil and gas in a rapidly evolving global energy landscape, fostering a more holistic understanding of the industry‚Äôs future direction.

By participating in the Company Visit, students not only expand their professional network but also gain a clearer perspective on how their academic background can contribute to the industry. This program is a vital stepping stone for aspiring professionals, allowing them to build connections, gain inspiration, and develop a stronger foundation for their careers in the energy sector. As SPE UGM SC remains committed to fostering student development and industry collaboration, the Company Visit continues to be a key component of APECX, ensuring that participants receive a well-rounded and impactful learning experience.
