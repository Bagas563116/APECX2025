BUSINESS CASE COMPETITION

The Business Case Competition is part of APECX 2025 (Annual Petroleum Exhibition and Competition), which provides a platform for aspiring innovators to solve real-world industry challenges. The Business Case Competition consists of 3 stages, Preliminary stage, Seminal Stage, and Final Round. This year, we are proud to present the competition theme, "Transforming Industries: Smart Technologies for a Better Society." Through this competition, participants are encouraged to develop innovative business solutions that address these challenges. The goal is to create sustainable business models that not only embrace smart technologies but also ensure a balanced social impact, maintaining employment opportunities and fostering an inclusive industrial transformation.
