CAREER TALK

Career Talk is an engaging and insightful sharing session designed for students to gain a deeper understanding of career opportunities, particularly in the ever-evolving field of oil and gas technology. This event serves as a bridge between academia and industry, providing participants with valuable insights into the latest advancements, trends, and challenges shaping the sector.

Distinguished speakers, who are experts and professionals in their respective fields, will be invited to share their experiences, knowledge, and perspectives on the latest technological innovations within their companies. Through this session, students will have the opportunity to explore emerging technologies, industry best practices, and the skills required to excel in the oil and gas sector.

By attending Career Talk, participants will not only expand their knowledge but also gain inspiration and guidance from industry leaders who have firsthand experience navigating the complexities of the field. This event aims to equip students with the insights and motivation needed to prepare for their future careers, fostering a deeper understanding of how cutting-edge technology continues to revolutionize the oil and gas industry.
