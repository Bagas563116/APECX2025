AWARDING NIGHT

The Awarding Night is a special moment to honor the outstanding achievements of participants and mark the official closing of APECX 2025. The announcement and presentation of awards to the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners in each competition category, recognizing their hard work, dedication, and innovation. Furthermore, a series of performances designed to entertain participants and guests, adding a lively and celebratory atmosphere to the evening.

The Awarding Night is not only a moment of recognition but also a celebration of the shared experiences, challenges, and achievements throughout the event. It brings together participants, organizers, and industry professionals in a formal yet festive gathering, creating lasting memories for everyone involved. The Awarding Night will mark the end of an incredible journey of learning, collaboration, and competition.
