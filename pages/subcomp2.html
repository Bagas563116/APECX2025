PAPER & POSTER COMPETITION

The Paper and Poster Competition at APECX 2025 invites undergraduate students to address key challenges in the oil and gas industry. The competition features preliminary stages, semifinals, and finals, with participants producing high-quality research papers and posters that present innovative solutions. The theme, "Unleashing Technology Advancements to Navigate the Multifaced Energy Trilemma in the Oil and Gas Industry," encourages participants to explore how technological advancements can enhance energy security, environmental sustainability, and economic competitiveness. The competition aims to inspire innovative approaches and contribute to a sustainable energy future for Indonesia's oil and gas sector.
